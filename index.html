Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

1876-1948

Father of Pakistan

Muhammad Ali Jinnah, also known as Qaid-e-Azam, was a Pakistani politician and lawyer who is widely regarded as the founder of Pakistan. He played an instrumental role in the movement for the creation of Pakistan and is revered as a national hero by the people of Pakistan.Jinnah was a brilliant orator and a visionary leader who dedicated his life to the cause of the Muslim community in India. His unwavering commitment to the creation of Pakistan was a testament to his dedication and determination. He passed away in 1948, just a year after the creation of Pakistan, leaving behind a legacy that continues to inspire generations of Pakistanis.In summary, Qaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah was a visionary leader, a brilliant lawyer, and a champion for the rights of the Muslim community in India. His tireless efforts and unwavering commitment to the cause of Pakistan continue to inspire millions of people to this day.
